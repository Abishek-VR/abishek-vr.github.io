About Me

Professional Photo

I'm Abishek V R, an Aeronautical Engineer working as a Junior Design Engineer at Tata Hitachi Construction Machinery via Layam. With a B.E. in Aeronautical Engineering from Bannari Amman Institute of Technology, I've transitioned into heavy machinery design, bringing aerospace precision to construction equipment.

My current work focuses on sheet metal and FRP component design for exterior profiles, NPD projects including Mini Compact machines and EV machines. I'm experienced in VAVE projects, competitor benchmarking, and localization efforts that drive cost savings and improve manufacturability.

While I work in heavy machinery, my heart still beats for aircraft design. I enjoy working with advanced CAD tools, solving complex design challenges, and contributing to NCC activities. I believe in continuous learning and applying aerospace engineering principles to create innovative solutions across industries.
