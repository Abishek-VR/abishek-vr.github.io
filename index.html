About Me

Bridging aerospace engineering principles with heavy machinery innovation

🛠️

I'm Abishek V R, an Aeronautical Engineer working as a Junior Design Engineer at Tata Hitachi Construction Machinery via Layam. With a B.E. in Aeronautical Engineering from Bannari Amman Institute of Technology, I've successfully transitioned into heavy machinery design, bringing aerospace precision to construction equipment.

My expertise lies in sheet metal and FRP component design for exterior profiles, NPD projects including Mini Compact machines and EV machines. I specialize in VAVE projects, competitor benchmarking, and localization efforts that drive cost savings and improve manufacturability.

While my professional focus is on heavy machinery, my passion for aircraft design continues to inspire my work. I believe in continuous learning, advanced CAD solutions, and applying aerospace engineering principles to create innovative solutions across industries.
